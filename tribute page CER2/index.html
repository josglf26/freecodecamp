Liam Payne

British singer and songwriter, former member of One Direction

Here's a time line of Liam Payne life:

  • 1993 - Liam Payne is born on November 12 in Wolverhampton, England.
  • 2008 - Auditions for The X Factor at age 14 but doesn't make it past the Judges' Houses.
  • 2010 - Auditions for The X Factor and is grouped with 1D.
  • 2010/15 - 1D becomes a global phenomenon, releasing 5 albums and going on world tours.
  • 2016 - Signs a solo record deal with Capitol Records.
  • 2017 - Releases debut solo single Strip That Down, featuring Quavo.
  • 2019 - Becomes a father to his son, Bear, with Cheryl Cole.
  • 2020 - Releases debut solo album LP1.
  • 2024 - Liam Payne died at the age of 30.
